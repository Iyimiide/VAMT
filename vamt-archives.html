Life History of Victoria Aderonke Joose Adegbite (Nee Olasinde)

Victoria Aderonke Joose was born to the family of Late Pa. Solomon Oguntunji Olasinde and Late Mama Elizabeth Olatorera Asabi Olasinde on 18th February 1933 at Olowologa Compound, Idi- Agbon Oke-Aree, Iree in Boripe Local Government Area, State of Osun.

She answered her divine appointment to the Lord on the 10th of April 2014. It is impossible to talk about Aderonke without talking about her love and service to the Lord...
